Instructor Training

Stop Losing Knowledge When Staff Walk Out the Door

Swim schools face constant instructor turnover. SmartMoves ensures your training standards don't leave when they do.

The Reality of Swim School Staffing

  • Sky-high turnover Most instructors are seasonal or part-time — you're constantly retraining
  • Young & inexperienced staff Many instructors are teens or young adults teaching for the first time
  • No consistent teaching method Every instructor teaches differently — parents notice the inconsistency
  • Training lives in people's heads When experienced staff leave, their knowledge goes with them
  • Onboarding takes too long New hires shadow for weeks before they can teach independently

How SmartMoves Solves This

Standardized Training Modules

Build structured training paths that every new instructor follows — from water safety basics to advanced stroke technique.

Achievements & Certifications

Instructors complete modules and earn certifications — track who is qualified to teach which levels.

Faster Onboarding

New instructors get up to speed in days, not weeks. Guided lesson plans mean they can teach confidently from day one.

Competency Dashboard

See at a glance which instructors are trained, who needs upskilling, and where your gaps are — across all locations.

The result? Your swim program stays consistent no matter who's on deck — and new instructors become effective faster than ever.
